Summary of Academic Integrity

In this module we have learned:

  • The definitions of academic integrity and academic misconduct,

  • Cultural considerations that are relevant to academic integrity,

  • Different types of academic misconduct including cheating, academic dishonesty, fabrication, re-use of work, and plagiarism,

  • How students can avoid academic misconduct,

  • What your responsibilities are as a student with regard to academic integrity,

  • The penalties that are triggered by a conviction of academic misconduct.
