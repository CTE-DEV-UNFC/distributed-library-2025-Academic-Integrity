Additional Resources for Students

If you would like to read more information about Academic Integrity at the University of Niagara Falls Canada, you can consult the Academic Integrity and Honesty Policy (which is policy number 1001). This policy can be accessed via the UNF website.

You may also access the UNF Library and Writing Centre support services. You may book a consultation with a Librarian or Writing Advisor to discuss research, referencing and citation, and paraphrasing and summary questions, which may help to keep you on track to maintain academic integrity standards.

Thank you for following along. We hope this resource has been helpful in educating you on Academic Integrity at UNF.
